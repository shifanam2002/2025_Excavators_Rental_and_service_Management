Excavator Safety Measures

Conduct a Pre-Inspection: Inspect the excavator for any damage, loose parts, or fluid leaks before starting work.

Wear Proper Safety Gear: Always wear a hard hat, reflective vest, steel-toed boots, and safety gloves while operating or working near an excavator.

Check the Surroundings: Ensure the work area is clear of obstacles, overhead power lines, and unauthorized personnel.

Use Seatbelts: Operators must always wear seatbelts while operating the excavator to prevent injury during sudden movements or accidents.

Stabilize the Machine: Lower the blade and stabilize the machine on a firm, level surface before beginning work.

Follow Load Limits: Avoid overloading the excavator beyond its capacity to maintain stability and prevent tipping.

Operate at Safe Speeds: Avoid sudden or jerky movements and operate the machine at safe speeds, especially in tight spaces.
